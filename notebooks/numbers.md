## Sets of numbers
Set of **{index}`natural numbers`** is denoted as $\mathbb{N}=\left\{0{,}1{,}2{,}3{,}...\right\}$, sometimes called as the "naturals".
Natural numbers without zero are $\mathbb{N}^+=\left\{1{,}2{,}3{,}...\right\}$, also called as "counting numbers".
